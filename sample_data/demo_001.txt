Company: BlazeSafe Fire Protection

Sales Rep: What systems do you use?

Client: We use ServiceTrade for job management. But don't create sprinkler emergency jobs automatically - our dispatcher handles that manually.

Sales Rep: Got it. Anything else?

Client: If a transfer fails, please tell the customer someone will call back within 15 minutes. That's our promise.
